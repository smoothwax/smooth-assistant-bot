OVERVIEW
Smooth Wax Bar is a waxing salon in London and Toronto Liberty Village. It is dedicated to waxing services, including Brazilians, backs, brows, toes, and everything in between. Smooth uses luxury imported waxes and trained Waxologists. The brand promise is fast, hygienic, virtually ouch-free waxing at a fair price.

WHY SMOOTH
Smooth emphasizes four main benefits:
1. Less Ouch: Waxologists are specially trained in techniques that reduce discomfort.
2. Cleanliness: Smooth is highly focused on hygiene. Waxologists never double dip wax sticks.
3. Priced Right: Smooth aims to provide high-quality waxing without luxury spa pricing.
4. Fast, Quality Service: Waxologists specialize in waxing and aim to provide quick, high-quality service.

PRE-WAX TIPS
- Plan waxing every 3 to 4 weeks.
- Do not shave in between appointments if possible.
- If the client has shaved, they should come at least three weeks after using a razor, otherwise the hair may be too short for waxing.
- Taking a warm shower before the appointment may help open pores.
- Skip lotion, gels, and oils on the day of the appointment.
- Some clients prefer to take ibuprofen 30 minutes before waxing.
- Avoid alcoholic beverages and sunbathing before the wax.
- Check skin before the appointment to make sure there are no cuts, moles, skin tags, or sores.

POST-WAX TIPS
For 24 hours after waxing:
- Avoid prolonged heat exposure, including sunbathing, intense exercise, and hot tubs.
- Avoid applying heavy creams, gels, or oils to freshly waxed areas.
- Keep the area cool and clean.

Ongoing care:
- Exfoliating helps keep skin smooth and free from ingrown hairs.
- Smooth carries scrubs, dry brushes, and PFB hair serum to help prevent ingrowns.

FAQ: CLIENT IS UNCOMFORTABLE BARING ALL
Waxologists are trained to make clients comfortable. They work quickly and cover body parts that do not need to be exposed. Disposable panties are available upon request.

FAQ: WAXING VS LASER
Smooth states that waxing is more affordable than laser hair removal. Laser hair removal is not suitable for everyone and tends to work best for clients with very light skin and very dark hair. It may not work well for darker skin/darker hair or very light skin/light hair. Laser can burn darker skin because it targets dark pigment. Laser is also not fully permanent; there can be 30-40% hair regrowth, and multiple expensive treatments are required.

FAQ: MALE CLIENTS
About 30% of Smooth clients are men. Male clients come for many reasons, including removing hair from the back or chest, athletic performance, or brow grooming. Smooth Waxologists are trained to wax men, and the environment is unisex.

FAQ: INGROWN HAIRS
Only medical professionals are certified to break skin. Waxologists will not remove ingrown hairs if doing so requires breaking the skin. Smooth offers solutions to help prevent ingrown hairs in the future, and the Waxologist can discuss these during the appointment.

FAQ: WAXING DURING PERIOD
Clients can wax during their period. They should wear a tampon and let the Waxologist know.

FAQ: WHEN NOT TO WAX
Smooth does not recommend waxing if the client has:
- a raised mole,
- varicose veins,
- acne medication use such as Accutane or Retin-A,
- corticosteroid use.

For staff: if a contraindication or medication question is unclear, follow the internal manual and ask a manager.

FAQ: SENSITIVE SKIN AND PAIN
Smooth waxes are formulated for sensitive skin. Waxologists aim to provide an ouch-free experience. For low pain tolerance or first-time waxing, Smooth recommends taking a pain reliever 30 to 45 minutes before the appointment or applying numbing cream.

FAQ: MISSED SPOT OR ASYMMETRY AFTER WAX
If a client gets home and finds that anything was not right about their wax, they should contact Smooth within 48 hours and Smooth will get them back in to make it perfect.

FAQ: MISSED APPOINTMENT
Smooth understands that things come up and will work to reschedule the appointment. After three missed appointments, Smooth will ask to hold future appointments with a credit card.
